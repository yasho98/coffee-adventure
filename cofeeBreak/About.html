Our Story

Welcome to Coffee Brake, where every cup tells a story.
We are a family-owned coffee shop that takes pride in sourcing
the best beans and crafting the perfect cup of coffee for you to enjoy.

Our story began when Mr. P. de Almeida fell in love with coffee during a trip to Brazil.
He was fascinated by the process of roasting and brewing and decided to bring that passion back home to Wennappuwa.
Mr. P. de Almeida started roasting his own beans in his garage and soon, word spread about his exceptional coffee.
The rest, as they say, is history.

At Coffee Brake, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink - it's an experience.
That's why we take care to roast our beans in small batches, to ensure that each cup is bursting with flavor.
Our baristas are passionate about coffee and are always happy to share their knowledge with you,
whether you're a coffee connoisseur or just getting started.

We also believe in giving back to our community.
That's why we source our beans from ethical and sustainable farms,
and why we partner with local organizations to support causes that are important to us.
We are committed to making a positive impact on the world, one cup of coffee at a time.

Thank you for joining us on this journey.
We look forward to sharing our love of coffee with you and hope that every cup brings you joy and inspiration.
